MAJA BLANCA

Home Recipes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups coconut milk
  • 1 cup cornstarch
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup whole kernel corn (drained)
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • Toasted coconut flakes or grated cheese (for topping)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine coconut milk, evaporated milk, condensed milk, and sugar. Stir until sugar dissolves.
  2. Add the whole kernel corn and mix well.
  3. Place the pot over medium heat and bring to a boil, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Gradually add cornstarch while stirring, until the mixture thickens into a paste-like consistency (about 10-15 minutes).
  5. Pour the mixture into a greased or lined dish and smooth the top with a spatula.
  6. Let the mixture cool to room temperature, then refrigerate for at least 1-2 hours until set.
  7. Top with toasted coconut flakes or grated cheese.
  8. Cut into squares and serve chilled.

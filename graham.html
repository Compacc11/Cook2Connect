GRAHAM CAKE (MANGO FLOAT)

Home Recipes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced
  • 1 can (14 oz) condensed milk
  • 1 can (12 oz) all-purpose cream
  • 1 pack of graham crackers
  • 1/2 cup crushed graham crackers (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Mixing the Cream: In a bowl, combine the condensed milk and all-purpose cream. Mix well until smooth and well combined.
  2. Layering: In a rectangular or square dish, arrange a layer of whole graham crackers at the bottom. Spread a layer of the cream mixture over the graham crackers. Place a layer of sliced mangoes on top of the cream mixture.
  3. Repeating Layers: Repeat the layers of graham crackers, cream mixture, and sliced mangoes until you reach the top of the dish or until youâ€™ve used up all your ingredients. The topmost layer should be the cream mixture.
  4. Topping: Sprinkle the top with crushed graham crackers for a nice finish.
  5. Chilling: Cover the dish with plastic wrap or foil and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight, to allow the cake to set and the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serving: Slice and serve chilled.

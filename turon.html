Turon (Banana Spring Rolls)

Home Recipes

Ingredients

  • 6 ripe saba bananas, cut in half lengthwise
  • 1 cup ripe jackfruit (langka), sliced into strips
  • 12 spring roll wrappers (lumpia wrappers)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Cooking oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Preparation: Peel and slice the saba bananas in half lengthwise. Prepare the jackfruit by slicing it into strips.
  2. Wrapping:
    1. Lay a spring roll wrapper on a flat surface.
    2. Place a slice of banana and a strip of jackfruit at one end of the wrapper.
    3. Sprinkle a little brown sugar over the fruit.
    4. Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling, then roll it up tightly, sealing the edge with a little water.
  3. Frying: Heat the cooking oil in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the wrapped turon until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per side. Once fried, remove the turon and drain on paper towels.
  4. Caramelizing: In the same pan, add the remaining brown sugar and a little water to make a syrup. Stir until the sugar is melted and turns into a caramel sauce. Roll the fried turon in the caramel sauce until coated.
  5. Serving: Let the turon cool slightly before serving to allow the caramel to harden. Enjoy while still warm for the best flavor and texture.

Flavor Profile

Turon offers a crispy and caramelized shell, with a soft, sweet banana and jackfruit filling that provides a delightful contrast of textures and flavors.

Sinigang na Baboy

Home Recipes

Ingredients

  • 4¼ cups water
  • 2 cups pork spare ribs, cut into chunks
  • 1 pc red onion, quartered
  • 2 pcs medium-sized tomatoes, quartered
  • 8 pcs sitaw, sliced into 2” pieces
  • 250 g kangkong stalk and leaves
  • 1 pc talong, sliced
  • 2 pcs siling pangsigang
  • 1 (20g) pack Knorr Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix Original
  • Optional: labanos, okra

Instructions

  1. Wash the pork spare ribs under cold running water to remove impurities.
  2. In a medium pot, add the pork ribs and 4¼ cups of water. Bring to a boil and skim off any scum that floats to the surface.
  3. Lower the heat to medium, then add the onions and tomatoes. Cover and let simmer until the meat is tender.
  4. Pierce the meat with a fork to check for tenderness and allow the flavors to seep into the pork.
  5. Add sitaw, kangkong stalks, and talong. Cook until the vegetables turn dark green, about 2–3 minutes. If using labanos and okra, add them as well.
  6. Add siling pangsigang, kangkong leaves, and the Knorr Sinigang Mix. Stir and simmer for another 2–3 minutes.
  7. Transfer to a serving bowl and serve hot with rice. Enjoy!
